4. Compute accuracy: rows where predicted_bucket === actual_bucket divided by total rows.
5. Compute LOO delta: for each row call `scoreWithoutSignal(row.signalScores, "ml")`, compute mean of scores without ml. Then mean overall_score WITH ml. Delta = mean_with - mean_without.
