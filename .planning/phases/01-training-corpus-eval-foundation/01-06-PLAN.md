<objective>
Migrate the corpus scraper from clockworks/tiktok-scraper to apidojo/tiktok-scraper (~12× cheaper at $0.30 vs $3.70/1K posts), run a broad scrape targeting ~25K raw items (~$5 Apify cost), compute empirical thresholds from the raw distribution (P90 viralFloor / P30 underCeiling per D-09), and seal the full.YYYY-MM-DD snapshot into thresholds.ts.

This plan is operationally-driven (NOT autonomous): includes a human checkpoint after calibration for operator to review threshold proposals and hand-paste the code block (D-13 immutability — no automated writes to thresholds.ts).

Strategy: Scrape ~25K raw (5 niches × 5000/niche), write JSONL cache, calibrate thresholds from distribution, bucket + upsert to training_corpus. Plan 01-07 runs v2.1 baseline on a 500-video stratified subset from the resulting corpus.
</objective>
