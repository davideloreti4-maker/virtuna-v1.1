## ‚öÝÔ∏è The load-bearing decision: an honest gauge, not an oracle

PRED-01/PRED-02 literally require a **"prediction-gauge"** showing probability + factors +
confidence. The Phase 5 UI-SPEC (Surface 2) **explicitly forbade inventing a meter or gauge**
to protect the bands-first honesty spine. **This spec resolves that collision rather than
contradicting it.** The two specs stay coherent because P6's gauge is the ONE *sanctioned*
gauge the milestone permits, and it is engineered to read as a **bounded, fuzzy,
panel-derived likelihood ‚Äî never a precise needle at a number** (CONTEXT D-01, D-05):

- **The band WORD is the hero** (Display 24): `Likely` / `Lean yes` / `Lean no` / `Toss-up` /
  `Unlikely`. Direction lives in the WORD, not in a pointer.
- **The range is a SOFT, FEATHERED SPAN** painted across a flat likelihood rail ‚Äî a *zone*
  ("somewhere in here"), with gradient-faded edges. **No needle. No single tick. No 0‚Äì100 dial
  with a crisp pointer. No center dot.** The feathering IS the honesty cue.
- **The range caption** (`~55‚Äì70%`) is the **ONLY sanctioned numeric** on the whole card, and
  it is **real panel signal** ‚Äî the min‚Äìmax of the analysts' individual leans, derived from
  aggregation (a planner guard must prove it never comes from a model field). It renders quiet
  (12px muted), never as the hero, always with a `~` tilde, always an en-dash range, never a
  decimal, never a single value.
- **Confidence = the tightness of that span** (D-05) ‚Äî rendered as a WORD (`High` / `Medium` /
  `Low`) AND reinforced by the span's width + its semantic data tone. One coherent
  panel-derived object (band center ¬∑ spread range ¬∑ tightness confidence), not three
  independent fields.

If you removed all color, the card would still read completely from its words and the
`~min‚Äìmax%` caption. That is the test the gauge must pass.

## Color

60 / 30 / 10 split, mapped to the flat-warm charcoal tokens:

| Role | Value | Usage |
|------|-------|-------|
| Dominant (60%) | `#262624` (`--color-background` / charcoal-app) | thread canvas behind the card |
| Secondary (30%) | `#1e1d1b` (`--color-surface`) + `white/[0.02]‚Äì[0.04]` lifts | the card surface, panel-drill rows, footer row; the gauge rail bg = `white/[0.06]` |
| Accent (10%) | `#d97757` (`--color-accent`, terracotta) | **ZERO on this card** ‚Äî see reserved-for |
| Destructive | `var(--color-error)` (`oklch(0.60 0.20 25)`, distinct from terracotta hue ~33) | hard error/refusal lines, person-SIM rejection nudge, the Low-confidence span tone |

**Accent reserved for (explicit):**
- **The `prediction-gauge` card uses ZERO terracotta accent.** Per the LOCKED near-zero-dosage
  rule (`DESIGN-SYSTEM.md` ¬ß"Accent dosage"), a screen with zero accent is the norm. The band
  word is cream. The Save check is cream. The gauge span uses *semantic data tokens* (below),
  which are **not** the accent. Do NOT reach for terracotta to make the forecast "pop" ‚Äî that
  instinct is the thing being removed.

**Band / confidence / data colors (sanctioned, NOT accent)** ‚Äî reuse the EXACT semantic tokens
from `band-block.tsx`'s `BAND_COLOR` map, never re-roll, never coral:

- **The band WORD (the gauge center) stays CREAM** (`text-foreground`). A likelihood has no
  inherent good/bad valence, so we do NOT paint `Likely` green or `Unlikely` red ‚Äî that would
  smuggle a value judgment. Direction reads from the WORD (`Lean yes` / `Lean no`).
- **Confidence is the one honestly-colorable dimension** (it answers "how much should you trust
  this") and maps onto the semantic trio ‚Äî applied to the **span fill** + the **confidence
  pill**, never the band word:
  - `High` confidence (tight range) ‚Üí `var(--color-success)`
  - `Medium` confidence ‚Üí `var(--color-warning)`
  - `Low` confidence (wide range) ‚Üí `var(--color-error)`
  - This mirrors `band-block`'s Strong‚Üísuccess / Mixed‚Üíwarning / Weak‚Üíerror = strength-of-signal.
    Here the "strength" is panel agreement. Span color and span width therefore BOTH encode
    confidence ‚Äî coherent, reinforcing, single panel-derived object (D-05).
- **For/against on factors** carries a WORD (`for` / `against`) plus an optional quiet tone
  (`for`‚Üísuccess-muted, `against`‚Üíerror-muted) ‚Äî never color-only.

**Borders / depth:** rest `white/[0.06]` (`--color-border`), hover `white/[0.10]`. Matte only ‚Äî
no glass gradient, no glow, no inset white-shine, no drop shadow on the card. Radius: card 12px
(`rounded-xl`, matches `reaction-distribution-block`), pills/chips full-round, the gauge rail
full-round (`rounded-full`). `--shadow-float` is NOT used on this result card.

**‚öÝÔ∏è Divergence from a stale analog (recorded, intentional):** `multi-audience-read-block.tsx`
paints a LEGACY coral `rgba(255,127,80,0.30)` Read panel (`READ_PANEL_STYLE`) that predates the
LOCKED dosage rule. **Do NOT copy it.** All multi-output panels on this card use a NEUTRAL
treatment (6% hairline + `white/[0.04]` lift + semibold lead) ‚Äî zero accent. (Flag F-03.)

### Surface 1 ‚Äî `prediction-gauge` card (hero, PRED-01/02/03)

Closest analog: `reaction-distribution-block.tsx` (the panel-variant card ‚Äî same provenance
header, same matte 12px shell, same collapsible drill, same `SaveAffordance` footer). The
`prediction-gauge` is its cousin. Mounts in `message-blocks.tsx` via `BLOCK_REGISTRY`.
Container: `rounded-xl border border-white/[0.06] bg-transparent overflow-hidden`, body
`px-4 pt-4 pb-3 flex flex-col gap-3` (verbatim from `reaction-distribution-block`).

Section order (top ‚Üí bottom):

1. **Provenance header** ‚Äî panel/audience name left (`text-[10px] font-medium uppercase
   tracking-[0.14em] text-foreground-muted`); right cluster `SIM-1 Flash` (12px muted) +
   `<TrustBadge tier="Directional" />`. Mirror `reaction-distribution-block` lines 54‚Äì63 exactly.

2. **Scenario lead** ‚Äî quiet `On: {scenario}` line (14px `text-foreground-secondary`, clamped
   to ~2 lines). The question, framed; not the hero.

3. **THE GAUGE (the hero) ‚Äî band + feathered span + confidence.** This is the load-bearing
   surface; build it precisely:
   - **Band word + range caption row** (`flex items-baseline gap-2`): the band WORD as Display
     (`text-2xl font-semibold leading-none text-foreground`) ‚Äî cream, NOT band-colored ‚Äî and the
     range caption trailing (`text-xs text-foreground-muted`, `~55‚Äì70%`). A `Confidence: Medium`
     pill sits at the row's end (full-round, `px-2 py-0.5`, text in the confidence semantic
     token, `bg-[var(--color-surface-elevated)]`).
   - **The likelihood rail** (below, `mt-3`): a full-width `h-2 rounded-full bg-white/[0.06]`
     track, conceptually divided into four equal zones left‚Üíright ‚Äî `Unlikely` ¬∑ `Toss-up` ¬∑
     `Lean` ¬∑ `Likely` (low‚Üíhigh likelihood-of-yes) ‚Äî with faint `white/[0.06]` hairline zone
     separators.
   - **The feathered span** (the honesty visual): a child `block h-full rounded-full`
     positioned from `left:{min}%` to `right:{100-max}%`, filled with the **confidence semantic
     token at low opacity** via a horizontal gradient that **fades to transparent at BOTH ends**
     (e.g. `linear-gradient(90deg, transparent, {confidenceColor}/0.28 18%, {confidenceColor}/0.28
     82%, transparent)`). The fade is mandatory ‚Äî it reads "somewhere in here," not "exactly
     here." **NO needle, NO center tick, NO pointer, NO dot.** Width visually = range tightness =
     confidence (reinforces the word).
   - **Zone labels** (below the rail, `mt-1`): the four zone words as 10px muted micro-labels,
     evenly distributed ‚Äî the only orientation the span needs.
   - **Single-point safety (F-01):** if `min === max` (analysts unanimous), still render a span
     (not a tick) by applying a minimum visual feather width (~10% of the rail) centered on the
     value, caption `~{v}%`. A unanimous panel is STILL not a point estimate ‚Äî keep the tilde +
     feather + a `High` confidence word.

4. **Factors ‚Äî the receipts (D-04)** ‚Äî section head `What's driving this`. Each `factors[]`
   entry = a driver line (14px `text-foreground` semibold lead + body detail) with **its analyst
   attribution chip** (`‚Äî {Archetype}`, capitalized, `text-xs`, `bg-[var(--color-surface-elevated)]`)
   and a `for` / `against` tag. **Every factor MUST name an analyst** ‚Äî no unattributed
   black-box driver (auditability is the whole point of PRED-03). Optionally group `for` above
   `against`.

5. **Panel composition ‚Äî who reasoned (D-04 honesty)** ‚Äî collapsible drill, reuse the
   `reaction-distribution-block` toggle idiom verbatim (lines 106‚Äì148): a `button`
   (`aria-expanded`) summarized `The panel ‚Äî {N} analysts` + `‚Üì Show`/`‚Üë Hide`; expanded ‚Üí each
   analyst row = archetype (capitalized, semibold 14) + their **qualitative lean WORD** (`leans
   yes` etc ‚Äî NO number) + a one-line reasoning quote (`blockquote`, italic 14 `text-foreground/70`).
   This shows the spread that produced the range ‚Äî the forecast is auditable, not an oracle.
   **Default collapsed** to keep the hero calm.

6. **Assumptions** ‚Äî section head `Assumptions`. The scenario's stated premises as a muted list
   + one lens line `Judged against: {success_criterion}`. NEUTRAL panel (6% hairline +
   `white/[0.04]`), never the legacy coral panel.

7. **Directional caveat** ‚Äî the D-04 caveat line, quiet 12px `text-foreground-muted`, ALWAYS
   visible (not behind a tooltip, mirror P5 F-04).

8. **Footer action row** ‚Äî top hairline (`border-t border-white/[0.06] px-4 py-3 flex
   items-center gap-4`): `<SaveAffordance item_type="read" title={...} snapshot={block.props} />`
   (cream) on the left; the optional `Run another scenario ‚Üí` neutral cream-secondary text button.

**Whole-card invariants:** bands/words only; the `~min‚Äìmax%` range caption is the SINGLE numeric
(the `.strict()` schema rejects any smuggled point-score; the UI must never render a single
likelihood value, a decimal, or a 0‚Äì100 dial pointer); zero accent; matte only.

## Non-Blocking Flags (grounded recommendations)

- **F-01 ‚Äî The range is the only sanctioned numeric, and it is never a point.** A planner
  guard/assert MUST prove the `~min‚Äìmax%` range is computed from aggregation of the panel's
  individual leans, NEVER read from a model field (D-01). The UI renders only `~min‚Äìmax%` (tilde,
  en-dash, no decimal). When `min === max`, still render a feathered span (not a tick) with a
  minimum visual width ‚Äî a unanimous panel is not a point estimate. Per-analyst drill rows show
  qualitative lean WORDS, never per-mind numbers (which would multiply false precision).
- **F-02 ‚Äî Feathered span, not a needle (the honesty visual).** No pointer, no dial, no crisp
  0‚Äì100 tick, no center dot. The span fades to transparent at both ends. This is the single most
  load-bearing visual decision of the phase ‚Äî it is what makes the gauge honest and keeps it
  coherent with the Phase 5 "no meter/gauge" rule (P6 introduces the ONE sanctioned, deliberately
  fuzzy gauge).
- **F-03 ‚Äî Confidence is colored, the band word is not; zero accent.** The semantic trio
  (success/warning/error) encodes confidence on the span + pill only. The band word stays cream
  (a likelihood has no good/bad valence). No terracotta. Keep `reskin-matte.test.ts` green ‚Äî no
  `rgba(255,127,80,*)` / `#FF7F50` / coral literals.
- **F-04 ‚Äî Caveat is always visible.** The D-04 Directional caveat is product copy, not a
  tooltip. Render it inline, quiet, on every `prediction-gauge`. Part of the trust contract
  (mirror P5 F-04). `TrustBadge tier="Directional"` + `SIM-1 Flash` always present (Predict is
  always Flash, always Directional by `resolveTier` rule).
- **F-05 ‚Äî Receipts are mandatory and attributed.** Every factor names which analyst raised it
  (D-04). No unattributed driver. The panel composition is always shown (collapsed) so the user
  can see whose reasoning produced the number ‚Äî never a black box.
- **F-06 ‚Äî Person-SIM rejection is honest, not a crash.** A person/non-General audience ‚Üí route
  **400 + inline warning nudge** (D-03/D-08), never a 500, never an `err.message` leak, never a
  toast. Folds the open P5 WR-03 route fix.
- **F-07 ‚Äî Minimal trigger only.** Chain-CTA on the Simulate card (+ optional minimal composer
  entry). No front-door picker, no mode menu, no ambient reactor (all P7). The creator composer
  path stays byte-identical.
