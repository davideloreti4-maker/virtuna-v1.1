**Structure:**
```tsx
<section className="py-24">
  <div className="max-w-3xl mx-auto px-6">
    {/* Section Header */}
    <div className="mb-12">
      <span className="text-sm text-foreground-muted">FAQ</span>
      <h2 className="font-display text-[40px] leading-[44px] font-[350] text-white mt-4">
        Common questions
      </h2>
    </div>

    {/* Accordion */}
    <Accordion type="single" collapsible className="space-y-2">
      {faqItems.map((item, index) => (
        <AccordionItem
          key={index}
          value={`item-${index}`}
          className="border border-white/10 rounded-lg overflow-hidden"
        >
          <AccordionTrigger className="w-full flex items-center justify-between p-4 text-left">
            <span className="text-white text-base">{item.question}</span>
            <CaretDown className="w-5 h-5 text-white transition-transform duration-200 data-[state=open]:rotate-180" />
          </AccordionTrigger>
          <AccordionContent className="px-4 pb-4">
            <p className="text-foreground-muted">{item.answer}</p>
          </AccordionContent>
        </AccordionItem>
      ))}
    </Accordion>
  </div>
</section>
```

**Styling:**
- Container: max-w-3xl (narrower than other sections)
- Item: border border-white/10 rounded-lg
- Question: text-white text-base (16px)
- Answer: text-foreground-muted
- Chevron: CaretDown from Phosphor, w-5 h-5, rotates 180deg when open

**Animation:**
- Height transition for content expand/collapse
- Chevron rotation: 0deg -> 180deg
- Duration: 200ms
- Easing: ease-out

**Accessibility:**
- Keyboard navigation (Tab, Enter/Space to toggle)
- ARIA attributes from Radix

Reference: `.reference/landing/faq.md` and `_assets/faq-viewport.png`
