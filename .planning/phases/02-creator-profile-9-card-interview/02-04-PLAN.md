| T-02-02a | E (Elevation of Privilege) — IDOR on profile update | profile-interview-store.persistCardData | mitigate | Store's `persistCardData` calls `auth.getUser()` inside the helper and writes via `.eq("user_id", user.id)` — never accepts a user_id from caller scope.
