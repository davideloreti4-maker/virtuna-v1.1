How It Works

From raw data to real understanding

Persona Creation

Personas powered by real data

We draw from multiple public sources to capture true preferences, motivations, and behaviors at an individual level.

Our engine distills these into lifelike personas, so your insights feel genuine and human.

Society Construction

Modelling how ideas spread through social networks

When you create a society, we retrieve relevant matches from our database of over 1 million personas.

We map how they connect and influence one another through a Social Graph - linking people by shared interests, backgrounds, and past interactions.

AI-driven Simulations

AI Personas powered by cutting-edge LLMs

Every experiment you run triggers personas who discover, share, and react to your idea.

Behind the scenes, hundreds of LLM calls process millions of tokens to capture both individual and collective behaviors.

Result Generation

Insights you can act on

See how personas respond to your ideas with both numbers and summaries.

Get data at the group and individual level - and chat with Amos, our social intelligence engine, for clear answers on your audience and results.

Automatic A/B Testing

Message variants, generated and tested for you

When you run a simulation, we take your original message and create multiple versions to test alongside.

Our variant writers incorporate your tone of voice, as well as best practice techniques for the message context.

FAQ

We simulated what questions you need answering

Explore quick solutions to common questions. Need more? Feel free to contact our support team.

What is an Artificial Society?

An artificial society is a digital simulation of a group of people - each represented by an AI “persona” with its own traits, preferences and behaviors.

How do credits work?

You consume one credit every time you run a simulation or regenerate variants. Free accounts have a starting balance of 3 credits and can start a free trial of the pro subscription after they have used them. Pro users have unlimited credits.

What kinds of experiments can I run?

Artificial Societies is designed for testing how messages, ideas, and concepts resonate with a target audience. Free and Pro plans have a set of pre-configured input ‘contexts’ which define how the personas encounter the information.

For example, these contexts include social media posts, emails, advertisements, and more. We’re continually expanding the range of contexts on offer so please reach out to us if there’s one you’d like to add.

What’s the difference between target and personal societies?

There are two types of society that you can create: target and personal. You create a target society by describing your desired audience in plain english.

Personal societies are built from the actual people who you engage with on social media, and those who engage with you in return. These societies place you, the author, at the centre of the network

How many personas are in a society?

Societies generally range from 20 to 300 personas based on how many profiles match your description. For larger societies or custom-builds, reach out to us at support@societies.io
